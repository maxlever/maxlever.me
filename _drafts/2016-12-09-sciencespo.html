In Fall 2016, I spent a semester in Paris studying political science and philosophy at Sciences Po. While studying there, I joined a Paris-based philosophy student organization called Opium Philosophie, and designed, wrote, and published communication material, including the posters below.






During my time at Sciences Po, I also joined a France-wide social enterprise student organization called NOISE. I organized, promoted, and facilitated a workshop on design thinking in my native French. I brought in a local entrepreneur and design thinking expert to help facilitate, and adapted an IDEO deck.








While working with NOISE, I also designed a logo for an incubator that supports entrepreneurs who are refugees.
