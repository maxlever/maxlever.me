DeepMoji is a project to understand emotional content, and nuances in language such as sarcasm and slang, by training a Machine Learning model with emojis. My role was to design the interface and develop a back-end infrastructure so that users can easily use the model.

Featured on: MIT Technology Review, BBC, Newsweek, The Telegraph, Huffington Post (FR)
