While on co-op at Ronik, I worked on a team that developed a responsive marketing site for Transforming Medecine, an NGO that matches people with life-threatening illnesses to the best treatments for their condition. I created the site using our own website scaffolding tool Slush Stencil, Stylus for styles, and deployed it to a Webhook CMS.

The website won two Horizon Interactive Awards.
