While on co-op at Ronik Design, I worked on a team that designed and developed a web application for Wellthy. Wellthy is a startup which provides assistance to people caring for their ailing loved ones by connecting them with caregivers. Working alongside Wellthy's back-end developers, I focused on developing the front-end interface for both customers and caregivers.

We started by creating a static version independent of the back-end using our own tool Slush Stencil for scaffolding; we styled the site using Stylus, and made templates using Swig. Once the back-end was completed, we integrated our pages into the Django templating system and connected our static site to the back-end.
