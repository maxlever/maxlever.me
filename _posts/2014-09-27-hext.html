Hext is a game I made with my team at the 2014 Global Game Jam in January. The topic was "We don't see things as they are, we see them as we are."

You play as a vain and ill-mannered human who has been hexed into a bat, and who must learn to navigate the environment using echolocation.

We coded the game in C# using the Unity Game Engine, and used Photoshop for the art assets and animations.

Project page: globalgamejam.org/2014/games/hext.
