Hext is a game we made at the 2014 Global Game Jam in January. The topic was "We don't see things as they are, we see them as we are."

You play as a vain and ill-mannered human who has been hexed into a bat, and who must learn to navigate the environment using echolocation.

Project page: globalgamejam.org/2014/games/hext
